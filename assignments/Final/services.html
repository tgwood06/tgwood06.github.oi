Special Services

Our specialized services are unmatched. They include:
free changing rooms and restrooms for temple patrons who have
traveled far and need to change into church clothes.
We have a playroom and a low cost, short-term babysitting service
for children of temple patrons. Our facilities offer a full business
center that supports family search and ordinance work.

Reception Services

We offer many special services for our hosted sealings.
We have a temple history themed reception and sitting area.
The reception hall is easily customized for your needs, and we have a
simple catering menu as well. We offer full kitchen access for foor
prep and serving. Our reception packages and amenities make your special day
low stress so that you can enjoy every moment.

Temple Missionaries

We have special accomodations for full-time temple missionaries
and temple presidencies. We have long-term stay packages that include in
kitchenette suites.
